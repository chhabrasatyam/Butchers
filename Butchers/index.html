Chicken
Briyani

Chicken Biryani is a savory chicken and rice dish that includes layers of chicken, rice, and aromatics that are steamed together. The bottom layer of rice absorbs all the chicken juices as it cooks, giving it a tender texture and rich flavor, while the top layer of rice turns out white and fluffy.

02.

Rawa
Dosa

Rava dosa is a thin and crisp South Indian crepe made with semolina, rice flour, spices and herbs. Rava is the South Indian name for semolina. Dosa is a thin Indian crepe. So rava dosa are nothing but semolina crepes. To flavor up the crepes, various spices & herbs like ginger, green chilies and cumin are used.
